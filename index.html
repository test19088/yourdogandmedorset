What We Offer

Our Grooming Services

The Full Groom

Double bath, full blow dry, brush and removal of dead coat, clipping/scissoring styled to your requirements, nails clipped, ears cleaned.

The Mini Groom

Double bath, full blow dry, brush and removal of dead coat, eyes cleared of hair, feet tidied, hygiene areas clipped, ears cleaned, nails clipped.

Bath & Blow Dry

Double bath, full blow dry and brush with removal of dead coat, ears cleaned, nails clipped.

Hand Stripping

A professional service where dead hair is pulled from the coat by hand. Includes double bath, full blow dry and brush, ears cleaned, nails clipped.

Grooming & Styling

Fully qualified and trained to cut to breed standard, while also following the latest grooming trends — round faces, flared legs, we'll meet your requirements.

Nail Trimming £10

Nails clipped and filed to keep them short and comfortable for your dog.

Nail Grinding £10

Our small grinding tool files the nails thoroughly after clipping, keeping them rounded and smooth with no sharp edges.

A groomer who actually cares about your dog
